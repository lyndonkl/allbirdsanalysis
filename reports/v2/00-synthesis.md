## Errata (added 2026-04-16)

Six corrections adopted after a 4-agent dialectical fact-check. Valuations are unchanged; the underlying inputs and the verdict distribution hold.

1. **Close price.** The April 15, 2026 close was **$16.99**, not $14.50 (which was the gain amount, i.e. +$14.50 on the day = +582% from the $2.49 prior close). Earlier intraday reference of $19.12 in some sub-reports was a mid-morning print. All per-share valuations in this corpus use 8.17M shares outstanding as denominator and are unaffected.
2. **Float vs. shares outstanding.** Public float is approximately **5.55–5.74M shares**, not 8.17M. The 8.17M figure is shares outstanding. Market cap at the $16.99 close is **$97M (float basis) to $139M (shares-outstanding basis)**.
3. **Preliminary proxy is on file.** The [preliminary proxy (PREM14A)](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1653909/000119312526155866/d39753dprem14a.htm) was filed April 15, 2026 on EDGAR — the same day as the press release. It discloses the convertible facility's existence including an **alternate floating-price conversion mechanic**. Specific formula parameters (VWAP lookback, discount percentage, activation triggers) may still require reading the facility agreement exhibits and the definitive proxy. The bear case's floating-conversion assumption is now structurally confirmed, not hypothetical. Dilution math in reports 02, 04, 06, 10 holds.
4. **S-3 shelf and ATM program exist.** Allbirds has an **S-3 shelf ($100M)** and a **$50M ATM program with TD Cowen**, both filed June 30, 2025 and disclosed in the FY2025 10-K. The ATM was used once (386,289 shares / $1.7M) through December 31, 2025. Reports that stated "no S-3 on file" are factually wrong; the stronger argument is that management had a clean financing channel available and chose a Chardan convertible instead on the day of the spike. Baby-shelf limits (Rule I.B.6, <$75M public float) cap incremental issuance proportionally.
5. **Implied bull probability.** At the corrected $16.99 close, implied probability of bull-case success is approximately **14%** (down from the 17% figure cited at $19.12 intraday). The 10-agent verdict dispersion is unchanged; no verdict crosses a threshold; the bear margins of safety strengthen modestly.
6. **Chardan precedents are structurally heterogeneous.** Bit Origin ($400M equity + $100M convertible debt), Classover (up to $500M senior secured convertible notes), Eyenovia / Hyperion DeFi ($50M PIPE of convertible preferred + warrants), VivoPower ($121M straight equity at $6.05/share), Lion Group ($600M ATW Partners facility). The recurring pattern is **"Chardan as placement agent or financial advisor for distressed micro-cap thematic-treasury pivots,"** not uniform toxic convertibles. The reference-class / base-rate reasoning survives; the specific-instrument inference tightens.
